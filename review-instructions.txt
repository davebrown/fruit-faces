1) Login with Facebook to to "Art for Breakfast" site. This is done by clicking the "Login..." link below the hamburger menu on the upper right.
2) Navigate to the "Upload" screen https://ff.moonspider.com/upload
3) Check the "Post to my Facebook timeline" checkbox
4) Click the "permission" button in the sentence "Please grant Art for Breakfast _permission_ to post to your timeline"
5) Accept the "publish_actions" permission
6) You may optionally describe the image you want to upload in the "Comment for your image" text area.
7) Click the button "choose photos to upload". Select a .jpg image.
8) Your image is uploaded to the Art for Breakfast site, and a description and link to your image will appear in your Facebook feed/timeline.
