Art for Breakfast Privacy Policy

Information Collection and Use

Our only goal in collecting personally identifiable information is to provide you with a way to share awesome fruit art photos - that's the heart of the Art for Breakfast product and service.

Information Collected Upon Registration

You can browse the Art for Breakfast site without registering with the site. To upload and share your own photos, then you must register. We collect the following information upon registration:

  • Name
  • Email address

Use of Contact Information

We will never share any personal information of our users with third parties, and we will not use your personal information for marketing purposes.

Cookies

Like many websites, we also use "cookie" technology to collect additional website usage data and to improve the Site and our service. We do not use cookies to collect personally identifiable information. Most Internet browsers automatically accept cookies. You can instruct your browser, by editing its options, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit.

Changing or Deleting Information

If you are a registered user of the site, you may access and update or correct the information you provided to us by e-mailing us at ff-support@moonspider.com.

Security

We are very concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your personally identifiable information. We employ administrative, physical and electronic measures designed to protect your information from unauthorized access. We will make any legally-required disclosures of any breach of the security, confidentiality, or integrity of your unencrypted electronically stored personal data to you via email or conspicuous posting on the site in the most expedient time possible and without unreasonable delay, consistent with (i) the legitimate needs of law enforcement or (ii) any measures necessary to determine the scope of the breach and restore the reasonable integrity of the data system.
